Who are we?

Kufa Foods Company is one of the best providers of broasted (fried chicken) product in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. We have more than 20 branches. We have developed a mobile application that makes it easy for customers to order their favorite meals, whether for home delivery or pick-up from the branch, without the hassle of waiting, in addition to many wonderful advantages.
